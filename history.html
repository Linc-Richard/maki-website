Our History

Since its founding in 1978, Maki High School has grown from a small community institution into one of the most respected schools in the Kilimanjaro region.

Milestones

  • 1978: School established with 120 students.
  • 1985: First national exam results ranked top 10 in the region.
  • 1995: Expansion of science laboratories and library facilities.
  • 2010: Introduction of ICT programs and digital learning.
  • 2020: Alumni network established, supporting scholarships and mentorship.
