About Maki High School

Founded in 1978, Maki High School has been a cornerstone of education in the Kilimanjaro region. Our mission is to nurture students with strong academic foundations, discipline, and creativity, preparing them to contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution recognized for excellence in academics, innovation, and community service.

Our Values

  • Integrity and discipline
  • Commitment to excellence
  • Respect for diversity
  • Service to the community
